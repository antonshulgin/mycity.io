What is MyCity

MyCity.io is a citizen engagement platform that helps municipalities and businesses involve residents in the decision making process in regards of urban infrastructure improvement.

In short, Mycity was made to help you with:

  • Collecting ideas

    Capture feedback that brings resident priorities into focus.

  • Building communities

    We help municipalities develop deeper connections with local residents. Engage residents, collaborate, build communities, inspire people to bridge the gap.

  • Implementing innovations

    Empowers you with the right tools for collaboration, implement innovations.
